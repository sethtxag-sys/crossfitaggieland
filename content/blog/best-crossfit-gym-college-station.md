## The Programming

We use Mayhem programming, which is designed by experienced coaches who've worked with thousands of athletes across different ages and abilities. This isn't some guy's random ideas posted daily. This is strategic programming.

What does strategic programming mean? It means:

- Workouts are designed to hit different energy systems (strength, power, aerobic capacity, work capacity)
- Volume and intensity are managed so you build fitness without burning out
- Movements progress logically—you don't jump from basics to advanced without the foundation
- There's a plan for the week, month, and year
- Your coach can explain *why* you're doing what you're doing

That's different from the gym that posts "AMRAP 10 minutes: do whatever you want as heavy as you can." That's not programming. That's just chaos that might feel good for a day but doesn't build lasting fitness.
